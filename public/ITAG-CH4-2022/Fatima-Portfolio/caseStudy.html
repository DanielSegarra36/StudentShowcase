Challenges

Some challenges I faced during the implementation of this project was making my space work for me. I also had to take into consideration pests, rodents, and transporting water.

Solutions

I utilized my vertical space as opposed to horizontal space to maximize efficiency. My DWC system reflected that as I installed plant supports to my bucket anticipating the vertical growth. As for the pests and rodents, I purchased a grow tent that would allow me to seal my system away. However, I wanted to give my plant as much natural sunlight as possible so during the day I would leave it open. I elevated my grow tent with a piece of wood for my own satisfaction and to make it harder for any critters to reach the grow tent. As for transporting water, I moved to a separate location which would allow me to get water fast and efficiently.
