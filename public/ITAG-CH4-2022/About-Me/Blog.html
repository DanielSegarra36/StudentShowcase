Agriculture

In Agriculture part of our training, we have learned about susainibility, sustainable food systems such as, hydroponic, aquaponic abd aeroponic systems that defined as closed loop systems. They are self-sufficient, energy and water saving systems.

Technology

Technology is important part of our training. We will use technology to set up our portfolio and website, and we integrate the sensor systems into our farmms.

Hands-On

In digital design class, we design our farms using sketch or canva programs. Measuring the grow area, considering plumbing, exits and entrances are important elements for designing our farms.

Construction

Construction part of our training is in person. We go to a farm and learn about carpentry tools and safety at the workplace. We practice using tools at the farm to build raised beds for vegetables.
