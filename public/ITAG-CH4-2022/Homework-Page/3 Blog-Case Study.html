Construction

Hands-on

It’s been years of my wanting to tackle the ‘big boys’ construction tools, and my dream has finally been realized. Along with all my fellow aspiring food enthusiast, I measured out, cut, and installed new outer wood panels for the plant boxes and propagate new plants. Our instructor, Alejandro, taught us about the work tools and we all quizzed each other on the names to memorize them perfectly. Below are the few tips and reminders we learned:

  1. Space out plant buds evenly.
  2. Pour a little water in the hole before plant bud.
  3. Keep plant from laying down to grown into ground.
  4. Fish head are good fertilizer. Just place in hole before planting bud.
  5. The numbers following a fertilizer name, correspond to the percentage of N, P and K elements–– nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium.
  6. Never power a saw while wearing work gloves.
  7. Never power on a circle saw when blade is placed directly on wood.
